Welcome

Explore how AI is changing healthcare in Canada – from diagnosis and robotic surgeries to future genomics and mental health tools.

Current AI Trends in Canada

  • Medical Imaging (e.g., X-rays analyzed with AI)
  • Virtual Assistants for patient interaction
  • AI-enhanced electronic health records
  • Robotic Surgery assistance

Future Trends (5–10 years)

  • AI-driven genomics and personalized medicine
  • Predictive diagnostics and early disease detection
  • AI-powered mental health tools and chatbots

Pros and Cons

Pros: Faster diagnosis, cost savings, improved accuracy.

Cons: Privacy concerns, job displacement, ethical risks.
